About Website

The Child Growth Tracking website is an online platform designed to help parents and healthcare professionals monitor children's development, focusing on BMI and key health metrics. It offers tools for parents to track growth and make informed care decisions, while providing healthcare professionals and administrators with features for managing information, offering consultations, and analyzing data to improve childcare and operational efficiency.
